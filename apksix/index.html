Frequently Asked Questions

Is APKSix free to use?

Yes, APKSix is an open-source tool and is completely free for everyone to use. We believe in providing powerful tools to the developer community without any cost.

What kind of modifications can I make?

You can perform a wide range of modifications, from simple resource changes like strings and images to more complex edits involving SMALI code and manifest adjustments.

Do I need to be an expert to use APKSix?

While some advanced features require a good understanding of Android app structure, our user-friendly interface makes it accessible for beginners to perform basic modifications.
